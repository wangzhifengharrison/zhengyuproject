Current Members

Lorenzo Di Michele (PI) [CV]

Lorenzo completed his undergraduate and master degrees in Physics at the University of L'Aquila (Abruzzo, Italy) in 2010, before moving to the Cavendish Laboratory, University of Cambridge to start his PhD in Soft Condensed Matter Physics. After graduating in 2013, Lorenzo took up an Oppenheimer Early Career Research Fellowship, followed by a Leverhulme Early Career Research Fellowship in 2016. Since 2018, Lorenzo holds a Royal Society University Research Fellowship at the Cavendish Lab.

Ryan Brady (Post-doctoral research associate, 2018 - ongoing)

Ryan completed his undergraduate degree in Nanotechnology at the University of Leeds, before joining the Cavendish Laboratory as an EPSRC CDT (NanoDTC) student in 2014, and DiMicheleLAB as a PhD student in 2015. He completed his PhD in 2018 and remained in the lab as a postdoc. Ryan focuses on the development of a new class of DNA-based nanostructures for the self-assembly of functional nanostructured crystalline frameworks.

Omar Amjad (PhD student, 2015 - ongoing)

Omar graduated in Biology from Imperial College London before joining 2014 the EPSRC CDT in Sensor Technology and Applications (Cambridge). In 2015 Omar started his PhD in the group of Pietro Cicuta at the Cavendish Labroatory, co-supervised by Lorenzo Di Michele. Omar studies the structure, dynamics and rheology of biomimetic tissue-like materials, as well as developing a new type of bio-molecular sensor.
